FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Why do I need this if Telegram already removes spam?
Telegram only blocks accounts from a global blacklist — those already caught before. A fresh account with casino ads in its bio looks perfectly clean to Telegram. That's exactly why modern spam works so well — it uses fresh accounts in both groups and channel comments.
How is this different from an entry captcha?
Captchas only filter dumb bots. A real person or a smart bot will pass any captcha — then spam for months. We don't block entry: we analyze the profile silently after the first message. Real users never notice anything.
Will the bot ban regular users by mistake?
No. At 50–90% confidence the bot doesn't auto-delete — it sends you a notification with "Delete" and "Not spam" buttons. Only at ≥90% does auto-delete kick in. False positives are rare — and when they happen, the "Not spam" button trains the AI not to repeat the mistake.
Is it hard to set up?
No. Add the bot to your group or channel's comment chat, grant admin rights — done. Takes 60 seconds. No registration, no forms. A step-by-step guide is in our channel if anything's unclear.
Does the bot read all messages in the chat?
The bot processes messages solely for spam checking. Chat history is not stored. The bot has no access to private chats — it only works in groups and channels where you've added it.
Can I train the bot for my community's specifics?
Yes. Forward any message to the bot in a private chat and write "Spam" or "Not spam". Each group gets its own knowledge base — the bot learns what's normal for your community and makes fewer mistakes.
